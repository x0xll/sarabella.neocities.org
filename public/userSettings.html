Settings Descriptions

"Horseshoe Multiplicator": Choose whether you wish to win horseshoes with the original amounts, or if you wish to multiply all horseshoe you win by 100.

"Map Play Page": Choose whether to default the game list to the text-based Play page (recommended for tactile devices), or to the custom flash-based map one (recommended for a slightly more original feel).

"Translated Quotes": Choose whether you want by default to have the Stables quotes in English or translated (may not be accurate to the original game).
